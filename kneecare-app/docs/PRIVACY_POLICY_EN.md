## 1. Information We Collect

KneeCare Companion stores the following information **on the user's device only** (on-device storage):

### Personal Information
- Name (optional)
- Year of birth
- Height, weight
- Affected knee side (left/right/both)
- Surgery date

### Health Information
- Daily pain score (0-10)
- Knee range of motion (ROM)
- Daily weight
- Swelling level
- Completed exercises
- Medication list and intake logs

### System Permissions
- **Notifications** — for medication reminders
- **Camera** — *Phase 2 only* for exercise pose analysis. Video is processed on-device and never recorded.

---

## 2. What We Don't Collect

- ❌ No GPS location
- ❌ No contacts
- ❌ No personally identifiable analytics
- ❌ No data transmitted off-device in current version

---

## 3. Data Transmission

**Current version (v0.1.x):** All data is stored on the user's device. No data is sent to any server.

**Future versions:** If cloud sync is added, we will:
1. Request explicit user consent
2. Clearly disclose what data will be transmitted
3. Use certified secure services (e.g. Google Cloud, AWS Asia-Pacific)
4. Comply with Thailand PDPA and TMI HAIT standards

---

## 4. Data Deletion

Users can delete all data at any time:
- Go to "Profile" → tap "Clear all data"
- Or uninstall the app

After deletion, data is permanently removed from the device and cannot be recovered.

---

## 5. PDPA Rights (Thailand)

Under the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (2019), users have the right to:
- Access their data (directly via the app)
- Delete data (directly via the app)
- Withdraw consent (uninstall the app)
- Lodge a complaint with the PDPC

---

## 6. Security

- Data is stored via the OS's AsyncStorage, accessible only to this app
- Users are advised to set a screen lock (PIN/Biometric) on their device
- If the device is lost, users should remotely wipe via Find My Device / Find My iPhone

---

## 7. Users Under 20 Years Old

This app is intended for adult patients with knee osteoarthritis or post-TKA recovery. It is not designed for children. Users under 20 require parental consent.

---

## 8. Medical Disclaimer

⚠️ **This app is not a medical device.** It is a supplementary tool only.
- The information and recommendations in this app **cannot replace medical diagnosis or treatment**
- For severe or unusual symptoms, contact a physician or hospital immediately
- Users use this app at their own responsibility
